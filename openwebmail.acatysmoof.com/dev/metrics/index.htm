Installation Metrics

When OpenWebMail is installed the systems administrators have the option of sending back helpful installation information to the developers. The charts on this page are generated daily from those gathered emails. They provide a glimpse of the popularity of the project over time. They also help guide development towards target platforms where support is needed most.

This information does not represent the metrics of OpenWebMail over its full lifetime. The information gathering that generates these metrics began 20060502 when OpenWebMail 2.52 was released.
